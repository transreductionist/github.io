Mercury Fell In love

Mercury fell in love
Tonight
With the moon.
Just
Like we do
Here on earth.
Beautiful
Full and silver,
Low
In the Western sky.
As violet robes
Turned blue.
How could he not!
And all this
Before
The Sun could
Wake up.
